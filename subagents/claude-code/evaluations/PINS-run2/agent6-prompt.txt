### Step 2: Overall Verdict

**Calculate percentage:**
```
Percentage = (Total Score / 130) × 100%
```

**Interpretation:**
- **100-130 (77%+):** Exceptional - Strong Buy
- **80-99 (62-76%):** Good - Buy at fair price
- **60-79 (46-61%):** Mediocre - Requires deep discount
- **<60 (<46%):** Avoid - Too many red flags

**Final verdict (decision tree):**
```
IF score ≥90 AND marginOfSafety ≥30%:
  → BUY (great business at great price)
  
ELSE IF score ≥80 AND marginOfSafety ≥10%:
  → WATCH (good business at fair price)
  
ELSE IF score ≥70 AND marginOfSafety <10%:
  → HOLD (decent business, wait for better price)
  
ELSE IF marginOfSafety <0%:
  → AVOID (overvalued regardless of quality)
  
ELSE:
  → AVOID (business quality too low)
```

**Buffett's rule:** Quality > Price, but BOTH matter. A wonderful business at a terrible price is still a terrible investment.
