## PHASE 3 — BUFFETT FRAMEWORK EVALUATION

Using all inputs gathered above, answer every section of Buffett's framework in detail. Display a clear output for each section.

---

### SECTION 1: BUSINESS QUALITY

**1.1 — Is the business simple and understandable?**

- Describe the business model in 3 sentences
- Rate simplicity: Simple / Moderate / Complex
- Buffett verdict: Would Buffett claim this is within his circle of competence?

**1.2 — Does the business have a durable competitive moat?**

- Identify the moat type and strength
- Rate moat: Wide / Narrow / None
- Evidence supporting this rating
- Key risks that could erode the moat
- Buffett verdict: Pass / Fail

**1.3 — Does the business have consistent operating history?**

- Has the company been profitable every year for the last 10 years?
- Has EPS grown consistently or erratically?
- Any major disruptions, losses, or restructurings?
- Buffett verdict: Pass / Fail

**1.4 — Does the business have favorable long term prospects?**

- Is the industry growing or shrinking?
- Will this business likely exist and be stronger in 20 years?
- What are the biggest long term threats?
- Buffett verdict: Pass / Fail
